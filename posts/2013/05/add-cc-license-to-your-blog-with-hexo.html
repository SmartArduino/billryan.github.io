add cc license to your blog with Hexo

Thank [@tommy351](https://github.com/tommy351/hexo) for developing Hexo-a fast, simple & powerful blog framework. In this article, he described how to use with Scaffolds. We can add some permanent information in it, such as the following Creative Commons license.

You can choose a license from creative commons’s site and put it under your scaffolds/*.md files. As for me, I put it in scaffolds/post.md.

B.T.W. Putting a CC license in your personal blog is a good habit. :) But… The Copyright Notice seems too obvious.
